What is a Rabbit Café?

Step into our inviting café space, then visit a dedicated bunny lounge filled with adorable, adoptable rabbits. Whether you're looking to meet your future furry best friend or simply want to unwind with some fluffy companionship, this is the place for you!

While cat cafés have grown in popularity, we’re bringing this unique concept to Lansing. A home for up to 25 rescued bunnies at a time, giving them love, care, and the chance to find forever homes.

Come sip, snuggle, and support a great cause!
